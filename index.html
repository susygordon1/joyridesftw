What is Joyrides?

This is where it all began.

Joyrides is Susy's calling — pure passion, purpose, and a whole lot of Holy Spirit packed into a car rolling through Fort Worth. She turns on the green beacon light, grabs the red megaphone, and pulls strangers into divine appointments. No subscription. No algorithm. Just call Susy, get in the car, and see what God does.

This is a mobile sanctuary — a mobile church, if you will. Passengers leave with prayers, hugs, a Baggie of Hope stuffed with mustard seeds and encouragement, and sometimes a Bible or devotional. You come as you are. You leave changed.
