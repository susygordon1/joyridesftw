What is Joyrides?

This is where it all began.

Joyrides is Susy's calling — a passion-driven ministry on wheels. No subscription. No paywall. Just a car, a calling, and whoever God sends through the door. Passengers receive prayers, devotionals, Bibles, and baggies filled with mustard seeds and encouragement. This is a mobile church, a rolling meeting room, a front-seat confessional.
